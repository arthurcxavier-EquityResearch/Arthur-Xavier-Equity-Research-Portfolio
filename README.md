Arthur Xavier – Equity Research Portfolio

Welcome to my Equity Research Portfolio.

This repository contains independent equity research reports and financial valuation models developed using publicly available information. The objective is to demonstrate practical skills in equity research, financial modeling, valuation, and investment analysis.

The reports included in this repository follow a structured equity research process similar to that used by investment banks and asset managers, including: Business & Industry Analysis Macroeconomic Assessment Financial Statement Analysis Financial Ratio Analysis Forecast Modeling WACC Construction Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Valuation Comparable Company Analysis Investment Thesis Risk Assessment

Each report is accompanied by a complete financial model containing: Historical Financial Statements Forecast Assumptions DCF Valuation Sensitivity Analysis WACC Calculation Comparable Company Analysis

Disclaimer All reports are prepared independently for educational and portfolio purposes only. They do not constitute investment advice.
